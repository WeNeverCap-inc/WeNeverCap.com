About iCup (Terminal's perspective)2

iCup1, created on 2020-06-03 at 9:48PM, was made as a joke to "rival" against the original cup server made by Wes. The server started as a joke but then grew into a real thing and as on 10 - 29 - 2020 it stands at 186 members currently. The server holds events often to interact with the community it's created. The server also features the cup channels, of crouse, but also features other side content such as community announcements and a memes channel based off of the original mems server also created by Wes. The channel was put in after the Pointless Company disbanded and people looked for an alternitive to keep up the cup legacy. It was a sad day to hear that they disbanded. But anyways, the cup army will live on with iCup and the cup legacy will continue. Long live iCup
